About Me

I am a graduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), affiliated with the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). My research focuses on 3D perception and scene understanding for autonomous driving, with particular interests in BEV perception, 3D occupancy prediction, and visual SLAM.
