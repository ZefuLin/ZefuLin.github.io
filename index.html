About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), where I was affiliated with the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). My research focuses on 3D perception and scene understanding for autonomous driving, including BEV perception, 3D occupancy prediction, and visual SLAM.
