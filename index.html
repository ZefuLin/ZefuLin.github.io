About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University. I received my M.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), where I was affiliated with the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). My research focuses on embodied intelligence — building agents that perceive, reason, and act in the physical world. I am particularly interested in world models for long-horizon prediction and planning, and vision–language–action (VLA) systems that connect perception, language, and control for generalizable robot behavior.
